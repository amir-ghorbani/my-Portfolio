Design Elements

I considered various factors during the design process, including accessibility, functionality, aesthetics, and readability. For instance, one of our customers was a hospital, and to ensure accessibility, I designed a separate interface for accessibility mode. In terms of functionality, I focused on incorporating features such as virtual tours and booking. Aesthetically, I aimed for a visually appealing UI design. Additionally, readability is critical in designing interfaces, paying attention to typography and font size.






Unity

I worked as part of a team with our developer to implement various design elements, drawing on my expertise in C# in Unity. These elements included screens, interactive features, and animations, all of which were created to enhance the product's user experience and aesthetic appeal.
